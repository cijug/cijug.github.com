Who We Are

CIJUG is a user group for application developers in the Central Iowa area. We meet the second Thursday of the month to discuss Java, JVM technologies, or anything else interesting (Javascript, NoSQL, DevOps, etc)

Where to Find Us

We usually meet at Businessolver. They are located at 1025 Ashworth Rd #403, West Des Moines, IA 50265
